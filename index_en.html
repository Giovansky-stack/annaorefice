The Bronze Doors

Sculptor Eduardo Filippo is the author of the new bronze doors. The reliefs of the Main Portal depict some episodes from the life of San Sossio, in a sequence of 10 panels.

The San Sossio Art Gallery

The Art Gallery is located in some rooms on the right side of the Basilica and houses silverware, canvases and panels from the 15th to the 18th century.
